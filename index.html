Heironeous' Guitar Case

Wondrous Item, rare (requires attunement)

Won in a duel between two fellow guitar players, one of which turned out to be Heironeous himself. It is engraved with a squiggly autograph.

Whenever you put a bow of any type in this case, the bow transforms into a guitar with the same effects.

The product of this case is considered a longbow. The damage is considered magical.

Bards can use these special longbows as a spellcasting focus as well.

Leira's Pebbles

Wondrous Item, rare

Small rocks from Leira's personal pond, imbued with all kinds of trickery and deceit.

This pouch contains 10 pebbles. When you throw a pebble on the ground, a small puff of smoke appears. Once per day, using a bonus action or reaction, you can throw a pebble at your own feet and teleport 15ft. to a location you can see. The remaining 9 pebbles you throw that day will only create the puff of smoke.

At midnight, the pouch refills itself back to full (10 pebbles).

Uriel's Flight

Gemstone, very rare (requires attunement, celestial blood)

Said to be refined from the moon and the purest of all light, this gemstone is blessed by the divine archangel Uriel himself. His power guides and protects those who share even the slightest of his divinity from the deepest and darkest plunges.

When the wearer of this ring is falling, spectral wings sprout from their back, allowing them to safely float down.

While floating, the wearer can move in any direction at will for a distance equal to the distance they are falling.

The Sapphire Droplet

Eldath's Tear

Amulet, very rare

Said to be forged at the center of an infinite whirlpool in the middle of the Quana Sea by Eldath herself, this droplet-shaped sapphire holds great power, and great amounts of water.

When you cast a spell using this amulet as a focus, the amulet fuses with your hand and you enter a new, calm state: Sapphire Peace. In this state your body is able to channel large amounts of water, as well as being able to resist pain.

While in Sapphire Peace, you can bring forth large amounts of water, making it possible to cast all spells that use water to be cast freely. You also gain +1 AC.

You can exit Sapphire Peace whenever you want but when you do, you lose your AC bonus and take damage equal to your level times 2. You may roll a constitution save to halve this damage (DC of 12).
